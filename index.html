📋 How to Play Brain-Freeze

  • 🎯 Objective: Name as many correct answers within a football category as possible. Name ALL of them and you beat the game!
  • 📁 Categories: Choose from many football topics — players in leagues, World Cup winners, top scorers, and more.
  • ✅ Answering: Type your answer and press Enter or click Submit. Spelling doesn't need to be perfect — close matches count!
  • 🔁 Already Guessed: If you type something already answered, you'll be told. It does NOT count as a wrong answer.
  • 🏳️ Passing: In solo mode, press "Pass / Give Up" to end and see all answers. In multiplayer, press "Resign" to give up your turn.

👤 Solo Mode

  • Name as many correct answers as you can. You can keep guessing freely.
  • Enable a time limit for extra challenge — when time runs out, the game ends.
  • When you're done, press "Pass / Give Up" to see all the answers you missed.
  • Name every single answer = YOU BEAT THE CATEGORY! 🏆

👥 2–4 Player Mode

  • Players take turns in order. Each turn, the active player must name a new correct answer.
  • If you cannot answer or choose to Resign, you are eliminated.
  • With a timer: each player gets their own countdown per turn. Running out of time = eliminated.
  • Last player remaining is the Winner! If all answers are found, remaining players all win.
  • In 2-player mode: the last player standing wins immediately when the other resigns or times out.

⏱️ Time Pressure

  • Choose 30s, 1 min, or 2 min per turn during setup.
  • Solo: the timer runs from the start of the game — answer as many as you can.
  • Multiplayer: each player gets a fresh timer on their turn.
  • Timer turns orange below 15 seconds, red below 5 seconds.
